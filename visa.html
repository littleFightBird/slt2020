Get a Shenzhen 5-Day Visa on Arrival!

  • Citizens of most countries can apply for a Shenzhen visa on arrival (VOA)
  • 5-Day VOA is available at 5 offices
  • Valid passport, complete the application form, take a photo and get 5-Day VOA within 10 mins
  • Costs 168 CNY or 304 CNY, depends on your nationality
